---
poem_title: "Hatter's riddle."
authors: phoenix
books: cobwebs-experiments
text: |   
   - Why a raven is like a writing desk?
   1. Because their produce is unpleasant?
   2. Because on the inside you may find an egg?
   3. Because of the black ink spots?
   4. Because the drawer is about to croak?
   5. Because both gave flying a try?
   6. Because quills have feathers?
