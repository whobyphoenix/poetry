text: |
   This constant pain is so distracting.
   I'm going through the list of old recipes,
   Time-proven remedies
   And I don't even know if I need to.
   I've kind of flipped,
   Decided
   To assume a guess
   In the absence of evidence.
   Perhaps,
      this is stupid, too.
