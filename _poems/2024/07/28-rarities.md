---
poem_title: "Rarities."
authors: phoenix
books: flashlight-in-the-dark
---

It's funny, how many 
Four-leaves clovers are there... 
One would think they are considered lucky 
'Cause they are rare...  
It's strange because
They follow me around 
This summer, 
And also rainbows,
Foxes, 
And thunderstorms.
