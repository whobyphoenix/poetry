---
poem_title: "Mudhamate."
authors: phoenix
books: unmeltable-mist
text: |
  My foolish heart
  Doesn't know how to sing.
  My foolish heart
  Can't stop singing.
  My foolish heart
  Doesn't know how to rhyme.
  My foolish heart
  Goes at it without rhyming.
  My foolish heart
  Doesn't know how to stop.
  My foolish heart
  Can't stop singing.
---
