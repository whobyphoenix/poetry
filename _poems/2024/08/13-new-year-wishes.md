  This year you fulfill my wish so precisely:
  Allowed me to meet my demons
  And to fight them.
  Now I know their faces so well:
  That of cruelty, that of cowardice.
  Two others are more like angels:
  One is trying to give up,
  Another - to give in,
  And both are failing.
