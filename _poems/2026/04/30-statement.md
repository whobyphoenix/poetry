   Война
   не кончится
   пока жив Путин.
   Война,
   что губит три славянских народа,
   три славянских страны,
   не кончится, пока Путин жив.
   Когда-то у нас был
   один на всех Гагарин,
   одно на всех девятое мая.
   Теперь - чёрный ужас один на всех.
   И единственный путь наверх -
   на свет -
   блокирует этот один человек.
   Да, можно ждать милостей от природы,
   но зачем?

   Law
   can work
   if and only if
   there is Law Enforcement -
   officers with the mandate, will, and means
   to stop violent crimes with violence.
   Humanity has its Consitution -
   the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.
   It emphasizes that human rights are universal
   and should be protected everywhere,
   regardless of borders.
   The UN does occasionaly wear a hat of a Law Enforcer
   but it's mostly about the war crimes, the "peacekeeping missions",
   and never when crimes are committed
   by governments represented in the UN Security Council.
   Law is a human concept,
   a shared hallucination -
   like gods and nations,
   like vows and borders.
   Such concepts can be real in their consequences.
   A shared hallucination holds
   only as long as the majority believes in its power,
   and those who undermine it are punished without exception.
   So. I'll say it again.
   Law
   can work
   if and only if
   every criminal believes
   that his crime will be investigated and prosecuted,
   that prosecution is inevitable,
   that there is Law Enforcement with the mandate, will, and means
   to stop his violent crime with violence.
