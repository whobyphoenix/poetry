---
poem_title: "Hidden under triple spells, charmed invisible, written backwards, buried under seventeen protection charms and invisible ink."
authors: phoenix
books: tunnel-vision
text: |
   My potion turns scarletty haze,
   My face burns with crimson dismay.
   Can't focus at all,
   When he strolls through the hall...
   My senses they all run away.
